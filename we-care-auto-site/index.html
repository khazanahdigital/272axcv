About We Care Auto

A Proud Member of M&B Auto Group

We Care Auto was founded in 2005 by John Mitchell, a certified master technician with over 20 years of experience in the automotive industry. John's vision was to create an auto repair shop that combined technical expertise with exceptional customer service.

What started as a small two-bay garage has grown into a full-service auto repair facility with state-of-the-art equipment and a team of ASE-certified technicians. In 2015, We Care Auto became part of the prestigious M&B Auto Group, allowing us to expand our services while maintaining our commitment to quality.

Today, We Care Auto is proud to be one of the most trusted names in automotive repair in the region, serving thousands of satisfied customers each year as a key member of the M&B Auto Group family.
